Ending youth homelessness

Photo by Ev on Unsplash

Every day, the homeless population is rising in Washington state. With limited amounts of shelters and health centers, people are left to survive in unsafe and inhumane environments. Unfortunately, a big amount of the homeless population consists of youths. There are many extra challenges that homeless youths have to deal with when they are not provided with a stable home. It is very heart wrenching to see that young people are not being given the proper care they need, while they make a very important transition into their adult life. The purpose of this website is to spread awareness and advocate for youths and young adults who are experiencing homelessness.
